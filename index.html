About

Active listening
for clarity.

Safe · Confidential · Not therapy

Sometimes you don't need a solution. You need someone to actually hear you, without the weight of being managed, without advice you didn't ask for, without performing okayness for someone who cares about you too much to just listen.

Jlisten2me offers structured one-hour active listening sessions by phone, video, or in person at a coffee shop in Austin. I hold space. You talk. That's the whole thing.

I'm JFeelgoodOfficial, an indie developer and artist based in Austin. I built this because I've been on both sides of conversations where nobody was really listening, and I know what it feels like when someone is.

There's a kind of relief that only comes when you stop managing how you're being perceived and just say the thing out loud.
